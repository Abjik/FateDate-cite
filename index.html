Find friends with similar interests

Our application is a social interaction platform based on shared interests. The main mechanism resembles the swipe system used in Tinder. Additionally, we drew inspiration from the concept of Omegle, offering random video connections. The unique feature of our application is that we strongly emphasize shared interests when matching users.
